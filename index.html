This is also about art

A book that explores the course of the last three decades of art. Never before had so much contemporary art been produced or sold. We are immersed in a boom of contemporary art and it's different expressions as a faithful reflection of what today's society is living. An art that dominates artistic expressions originating from the 20th century, coinciding with the rupture of traditional models and a new critical and experimental character. The layout of the book, with prominent white space, maintains a clear essence by using a single typography as the primary element to convey the project's personality through its content.
