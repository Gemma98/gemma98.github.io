About
I am a Creative Designer, growing up in a small town in the southwest near Barcelona, surrounded by vineyards and green countryside.

I studied Graphic Communication and have lived in Germany and the Netherlands, gaining experience as both a designer and strategist. Bringing projects and brands to life through thoughtful design and digital experiences, my work spans product strategy, visual design, brand identity, web design, and interface prototyping.

I believe design thrives when it draws from diverse fields, becoming a tool for storytelling, overcoming challenges, and driving meaningful change for a better future.
